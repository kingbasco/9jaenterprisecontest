Who we are

The Ondo City enterprise contest is a proposed annual entrepreneurial competition targeted to provide empowerment for Ondo youths and young adults between age of 18 to 45 who are entrepreneurs or intending entrepreneurs.


The contest will provide capacity building, boardroom session, bootstrapping, funding, connections to investors, market access, promotions and access to shared facilities.


Startups and small business owners find it difficult accessing loans from bank because their enterprise lack structure and has not stood the test of time. Some does not have tangible assets as collateral. What they have is just idea and picture which can be term as soft assets, most creditors are not interested as this cannot be used as collateral for loans.


The enterprise contest is an avenue for serious entrepreneurs to get funding using their soft assets by been able to defend their ideas and create the big picture of the future to the mentors. This contest will be an eye opener for most youths as the have the opportunity to get their business drilled and this will also assist them in pitching their business beyond the contest.



A key section of the enterprise contest involves capacity building for two weeks focusing on starting and growing enterprise. The contestants shall be camped and their business plans/models shall be properly scrutinized by the mentors.


Sticking to the theory of comparative advantage which states that countries (or indeed kingdom) will maximize returns in the form of increased income by specializing in the production of goods and services that are relatively intensive in the use of inputs in which the country/kingdom is well endowed. The enterprise contest will also be used to explore our area of comparative advantage; a special prize has been dedicated to promoting indigenous business.

Ondo City Enterprise Contest
Our Mission

Ondo City enterprise contest envisions providing adequate capacity building and funding to youth in Ondo kingdom so they can identify opportunity gaps for their holistic empowerment.

01
Our Vision

To raise entrepreneurs and enterprises capable of competing in the global market.

02
Our Objectives

To harness full potential of the youth in the community in order to strike a judicious balance between their quality of life as well as meaningful contribution to the community by raising at least 200 successful entrepreneurs by year 2025.
