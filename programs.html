1. Online submission of business concept.

An online platform will be dedicated for accepting participantsâ€™ business concepts to check viability of the project. What will be required here is just a mini summary of the entire concept. Participants that are not fluent in English shall be allowed to do a Yoruba language and Ondo dialect presentation.


2. Invitation for physical interview

From the information gathered from the online platform, entrepreneurs with viable concept will be invited for physical interview and selection for entry into the contest.


3. Boot camping and submission of business proposal

50 entrepreneurs shall be selected for boot camping where they shall be inducted into the entrepreneurship program. They shall be exposed to basic entrepreneurial training for 3 days which will focus on the following:

  • Ideas and Opportunities Development
  • Business Expansion Strategies
  • Introduction to Business Development and Management
  • Writing of Business Plan and Proposal

4. The enterprise camping

Twenty best business proposal shall be selected from the boot camping and the entrepreneurs shall proceed to the enterprise camp. Participants shall be camped for two weeks. Facilitators and mentors will be available and it shall be broadcasted through media platforms for viewers to also make comment.


5. Grand Finale

An event will be organized for the presentation of prizes and awards of recognition to participants, sponsors, mentors, facilitators and planning committee members not later than a week after the enterprise camping.


6. Monitoring And Evalaution

Winners from the contest will be presented with a board cheque but the actually disbursement of the fund will come in stages according to the financial plan in the business proposal of the winners. This will prevent diversion of funds. A team will also be set up to monitor and evaluate the progress of the winners in case there are challenges. Winners will also have access to the academy monthly business clinic.
