Meet our Mentors and Facilitators

Chief Oyeneyin Hakeem Oladipo

Hakeem Oladipo Oyeneyin is a graduate of IFS (Institute of Financial Studies) University College London and an associate member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers of England and Wales. He also holds a Masters of Business Administration degree of the Obafemi Awolowo University, Ife. He was a credit analyst with Cooperative Bank, London. Hakeem attended many courses in supply chain including Strategies and Leadership in Supply Chain at the Stanford Graduate School of Business, Stanford University. Hakeem is the founder of Visible Value Chain Limited and the predecessor companies that has evolved into a major player in Nigeria’s supply chain industry. Hakeem is the founder of Ileri Oluwa House of Hope, a foundation providing free basic Primary Care for Widows and Aged in Ondo Community.

Prof. Femi Mimiko

Professor Nahzeem Oluwafemi Mimiko was born on May 1, 1960. He holds B.Sc. Social Sciences (Political Science), 1982; M.Sc. and PhD International Relations (1984 and 1992 respectively), from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria. He did post-doctoral studies at The Thomas Watson Institute for International Studies, Brown University, Providence, Rhode Island, USA, in 1993; and The Academy of Korean Studies, Seoul, South Korea, in 1996. Mimiko was a participant on the Senior Executive Course (SEC) 37, 2015, of Nigeria’s most prestigious think-tank, the National Institute for Policy and Strategic Studies, Kuru, by virtue of which he holds, Membership of the National Institute (mni). He served as the fourth Vice Chancellor of Adekunle Ajasin University, from 2010 to 2015. On Prof. Mimiko’s watch, AAUA was rated by the US Transparency International Standard (USTIS) as the best State University in Nigeria, 2013. Femi Mimiko has served as Consultant on development issues to a number of local and international agencies.

Sir chief Ayodele Akinrimisi Akingbule

A native of Ondo. Attended University of Ife ( now Obafemi Awolowo University) to study Economics with specialization in Accountancy. In his quest for further educational laurels, he enrolled with the Nigeria Institute of Management and obtained the Institution's Diploma in October 1987 and was admitted as full member of the Nigerian Institute of Management (MNIM) in 1988 He attended Obafemi Awolowo University Ile Ife between 1988 and 1989 for his Masters degree in Business Administration (MBA) He enrolled with the Institute of Cost and Management Accountants London in 1988 He passed his professional examination in 1992. He was conferred Honourary Doctorate Degree in Public Administration in 2006 by the Edexcel University Benin Republic. He is director to many companies. Some of which are Fundaak Nig Ltd, Balogun Ayo Farms, Fundaak Agro Allied and Financial Management Consultants, Sir Ayo Feedmill, Director FARO GROUP, Vice Chairman Ekinmogun Micro Finance Bank Ltd, Ondo.,

Ms. Olusola Sowemimo

Olusola Sowemimo holds a B. Law (LLB) from the University of Lagos. After her undergraduate studies, she proceeded to the Nigerian law school. She is CIPD certified ( UK). A trained lawyer with numerous experiences. Currently she is a partner at Seyi Sowemimo and co, she has brought the best out of people through business and social etiquette as a certified etiquette consultant, executive coach, certified trainer at Queenelle consulting. She has worked as a management coordinator, a legal assistant and a human resource personnel at various organizations including Nigeria LNG limited, UTC group. Olusola’s passion for a healthy lifestyle created another entrepreneurial engagement for her to become an organic farmer which she founded a certified organic farm called Ope farms. Ope farms produces vegetables with no chemical additives. She is a faculty member at school of eloquence where she has developed people to speak with power.

Dr. Nelson Osungbemiro

Dr. Nelson is a great inpiration to Ondo youths. Rising from a humble begiining to prominence in both academic and Business world. Dr. Nelson is a Chief Lecturer at the department of Biology, Adeyemi Federal University of Education. He has a PhD in fisheries mangement from the Federal Univeristy of Technology, Akure. He is an Aquaculture and Fisheries Consultant. A real estate developer and the founder of First Victoria city county. He’s is the Vice chairman of the youth Advisory committee to the Ondo Development committee. He also serve on the Board of directors fo EKI FM.

Chief Wole Benson

Wole has a Bachelor degree in Agric Economics from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria and Post Graduate Diploma in Mass Communications from University of Lagos. He has vast experience spanning 25 years as a Media Communications and Branding Strategist, having worked as the MD of Mediamatryx, and Group Head of Media at McCann Erickson WorldGroup, Franchise Communications and Ogilvy, Benson & Mather, Nigeria.He is currently the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Cerebral Consult, a Media Communications firm based in Lagos, Nigeria. He is also the Chairman of TitanicView, a branding agency in Lagos, Nigeria. He is the pioneer Dean of Faculty of Media at Orange Academy – a Brand Building Institute in Lagos, Nigeria.

Dr. Mrs. Bridget Awosika

Dr. Mrs. Bridget Awosika is a well known motivational speaker in Ondo kingdom. Her passion for youth development is second to none. Chief lecturer at adeyemi Federal University of Education, Ondo. She studied Home economics from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife and also holds a Phd. in Textile and Clothing. She’s a member, Internationational federation for Home economics (IFHE), member, Housing Education Research Association (HERA) and Fellow, National Institute of Administration. She’s a founding member, Project Afrika De colonial and Indigenous knowledge (PADIK). She’s the chairperson youth adivisory committee to the Ondo Development Committee.

Mr. Mamukuyomi Idris

Mr. Idris sits atop many businesses and these includes; the CEO of Afriexporter.com, a dynamic and diversified E-Business with a portfolio of export promotions in regional and international markets under Heeds & Field Resources Ltd; President and CEO of Heeds and Field Resources Ltd (HFR), Regional Executive Director of Afriexporter Academy (afriexporteracademy.com), Founder of Afriexporter International Expo. He has featured in the annual events of the following organisations as a resource person:
• Nigerian-American Chamber of Commerce African Food and products Exhibition and conference.
• Zenith Bank’s zero to hero export seminar
