WHO WE ARE

The Ondo City enterprise contest is an annual entrepreneurial competition targeted to provide empowerment for Ondo youths and young adults between age of 18 to 45 who are entrepreneurs or intending entrepreneurs. The contest will provide capacity building, boardroom session, bootstrapping, funding, connections to investors, market access, promotions and access to shared facilities. Participants will have the opportunity to boost their businesses with One Million Naira seed funding.

  • Our Mission

    Ondo City enterprise contest envisions providing adequate capacity building and funding to youth in ondo kingdom so they can identify opportunity gaps for their holistic empowerment.

  • Our Vision

    To raise entrepreneurs and enterprises capable of competing in the global market.

Ondo City Enterprise Contest Core Objectives
